ABOUT ME


I’ve worked as a Character Rigger and Rigging TD on various TV shows and films, contributing to projects at Image Engine Studio, Bardel Entertainment, Zoic Studios, and Mikros Animation Paris.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the chance to rig a diverse range of creatures and human models. What excites me most, however, is solving unique technical challenges. I’ve developed custom tools, improved rigging pipelines, and implemented solutions tailored to the specific needs of productions. I’m highly experienced with Python for creating tools in Maya, leveraging the Maya API and PySide. I also enjoy learning new software, programming languages, and processes to meet the demands of a project, something I’ve done consistently in the industry.

I hold a master’s degree in CG, Animation, and VFX from ESMA, Toulouse, where I received a broad generalist education in modeling, sculpting, rendering, rigging, animation, and compositing.
For my graduation short film, Missing Key, I collaborated with a team of five classmates, contributing to rigging, scripting, and compositing.

ABOUT ME


I’v worked at Image Engine Studio as a Rigging TD on multiple tv-shows and films, from 2021 to 2024.
I’ve worked at Zoic studios as a Rigging TD on multiple tv-shows and films, from 2018 to 2020.
I’ve worked as a Character Rigger at Mikros Animation Paris, on the animation feature film ”Sherlock Gnomes”, in 2017.

I had the chance to rig a lot of different creatures and human models, but more interestingly for me I also had the opportunity to do a lot of development work needed for specific creatures and shows, building specific tools and improving the rigging pipeline to fulfill the different needs of each production. I am very familiar with using Python to create new tools within Maya, using the Maya API and PySide. I also love having to learn new software, languages or processes needed to complete an asset or a production tool, as I’ve done many times during my time in the industry.

I got my master degree in CG, Animation, and VFX at ESMA, Toulouse in France.
I received a generalist formation including : modeling, sculpting, rendering, rigging, animation, and compositing.
I've worked with a team of 5 other classmate, on our graduation short film : "Missing Key", on which I've done, among other things, the rigging, scripting and part of the compositing.
